Implementing Quantum Computing Modules in Undergraduate Physics Curriculum

I am proposing a fundamental shift in our 3rd-year Physics curriculum to include a dedicated track for Practical Quantum Computing. As the industry moves toward quantum advantage, our graduates must be equipped with the theoretical foundations and the practical skills to program using Qiskit and Braket.

This aspiration involves procuring three high-performance simulation workstations and establishing a partnership with local research labs. The goal is to provide a "hands-on" experience that transcends the purely mathematical approach traditionally taught.
